Or it may have been generayted because some dog pople strongly dislike cats and some cat people disdain dogs. However, dogs and cats can live peaceably as long owners understend the behaviors of each.

In some cases, a resident cat will isolate itself when a puppy is added to the family. In other cases, cats ad dogs never get used to each other.In still other cases, cat or kitten and dog or puppy play together and buld a frindship that finds them curled up together in a crate or bed and drinking out of the same bowl.
